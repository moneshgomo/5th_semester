# Important Questions (Based on Previous Pondicherry University Exams)

1. Define Cloud Computing. Explain its layers, types, benefits, and limitations.  
2. Write short notes on IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS with examples.  
3. What are the challenges and risks in cloud adoption?  
4. Explain the role of Open Standards in cloud computing.  
5. Compare SaaS and PaaS with real-world examples.  
6. What is virtualization? Explain the role of hypervisors.  
7. Discuss Load Balancing in cloud computing.  
8. Explain VM provisioning and migration in detail.  
9. What are Cloud Management Standards?  
10. How is security ensured in the cloud?  
11. Short notes: Identity as a Service, Compliance as a Service.  
12. Compare Google Cloud, AWS, and Microsoft Azure.  
13. Explain Machine Imaging in cloud environments.  
14. How is "Establishing Identity and Presence" important in security?  
15. Case studies on AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure.
